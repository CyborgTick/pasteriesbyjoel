About Joel's Pasteries

In Business Since The Queen Died

Founded by Joel and his family, Joel's pasteries is known for being the best confectionary store around LA and Nigeria. Joel himself was responsible for designing the initial formula for the well known award-winning x-pastery. The famous Queen Elizabeth II enjoyed the amazing pastery and granted it her seal of approval before she passed on recently.
